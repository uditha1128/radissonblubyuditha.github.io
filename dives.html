Dive Site

  • Hikkaduwa Reef

    Located on the southwest coast, Hikkaduwa is one of the most famous coral reef sites in Sri Lanka. It's a Marine National Park known for its diverse coral species and marine life, including colorful fish, turtles, and crustaceans.

  • Unawatuna Reef

    Near Galle on the southern coast, Unawatuna is famous for its beautiful beach and coral reefs. It is a popular spot for snorkeling and diving, offering glimpses of colorful corals and a variety of fish species.tincidunt vel sapien.

  • Bar Reef

    Located off the coast of Kalpitiya in the northwest, Bar Reef is the largest coral reef in Sri Lanka. It spans over 300 square kilometers and is known for its extensive coral formations and diverse marine life, including dolphins and whales.
